Which of the following is a “sometimes” food?

(A) salad

(B) soup

(C) chicken

(D) ** doughnut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A doughnut or donut (/ˈdoʊnət/) is a type of food made from leavened fried dough. It is popular in many countries and is prepared in various forms as a sweet snack that can be homemade or purchased in bakeries, supermarkets, food stalls, and franchised specialty vendors.

Concept note-2: -Yeast doughnuts, as the name clearly spells out, are made from dough leavened with yeast (think brioche), whereas cake doughnuts are traditionally made from a kind of cake batter that uses a chemical leavener (i.e. baking powder or baking soda).

Concept note-3: -The glazed doughnut is a classic flavor you will find at any doughnut shop. This consists of sweet dough that is deep fried and then dipped into a sugary glaze until it glistens.
