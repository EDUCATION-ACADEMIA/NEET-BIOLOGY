lateral means

(A) ** farther from the body midline.

(B) more superficial

(C) towards the body midline

(D) closer to the attachment point of a limb

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Medial-toward the midline of the body (example, the middle toe is located at the medial side of the foot). Lateral-away from the midline of the body (example, the little toe is located at the lateral side of the foot).

Concept note-2: -A lateral orientation is a position away from the midline of the body. For instance, the arms are lateral to the chest, and the ears are lateral to the head. A medial orientation is a position toward the midline of the body.

Concept note-3: -Lateral means to the side of, or away from, the middle of the body. Examples: The ears are lateral to the nose. The arms are lateral to the chest.

Concept note-4: -The term distal means farthest from midline or ending or the structure.

Concept note-5: -The shoulders are lateral to the midline.
