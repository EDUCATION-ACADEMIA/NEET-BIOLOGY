Which food class provide the highest energy value?

(A) Carbohydtare

(B) Protein

(C) ** Fat

(D) Vitamin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Fats. Fats have the highest gross calorific value.

Concept note-2: -Fat: 9 Calories per Gram Fat provides the most energy of all the macronutrients, at a whopping 9 calories per gram.

Concept note-3: -Ethanol and fats have the highest amount of calorific value per gram i.e. 29 and 37 kilojoules per gram or 6.9 and 8.8kcal/g respectively and proteins and most carbohydrates both have about 17kJ/g or 4kcal/g.

Concept note-4: -Fats are the slowest source of energy but the most energy-efficient form of food. Each gram of fat supplies the body with about 9 calories, more than twice that supplied by proteins or carbohydrates. Because fats are such an efficient form of energy, the body stores any excess energy as fat.
