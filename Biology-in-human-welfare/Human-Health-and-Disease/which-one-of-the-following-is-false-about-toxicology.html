EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Toxicity is a general term used to indicate adverse effects produced by poisons. These adverse effects can range from slight symptoms like headaches or nausea, to severe symptoms like coma and convulsions and death.

Concept note-2: -The toxicity of a substance usually depends on the following factors: Form and innate chemical activity. Dosage, especially dose-time relationship. Exposure route.

Concept note-3: -The toxicity of a substance depends on three factors: its chemical structure, the extent to which the substance is absorbed by the body, and the bodyâ€™s ability to detoxify the substance (change it into less toxic substances) and eliminate it from the body.
