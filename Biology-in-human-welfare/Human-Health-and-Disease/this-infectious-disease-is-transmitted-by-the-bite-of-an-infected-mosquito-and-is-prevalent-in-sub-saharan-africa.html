This infectious disease is transmitted by the bite of an infected mosquito and is prevalent in sub-saharan Africa

(A) West Nile

(B) SARS

(C) ** Malaria

(D) Cholera

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites that are transmitted to people through the bites of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes. It is preventable and curable. In 2021, there were an estimated 247 million cases of malaria worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Diseases that are spread to people by mosquitoes include Zika virus, West Nile virus, Chikungunya virus, dengue, and malaria.

Concept note-3: -Only Anopheles mosquitoes can transmit malaria and they must have been infected through a previous blood meal taken from an infected person.

Concept note-5: -Chikungunya virus is spread to people by the bite of an infected mosquito. The most common symptoms of infection are fever and joint pain.
