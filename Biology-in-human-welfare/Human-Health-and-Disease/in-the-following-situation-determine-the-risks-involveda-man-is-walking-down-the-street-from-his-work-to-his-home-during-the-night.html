Concept note-1: -There are four parts to any good risk assessment and they are Asset identification, Risk Analysis, Risk likelihood & impact, and Cost of Solutions.

Concept note-2: -A hazard is a source or a situation with the potential for harm in terms of human injury or ill-health, damage to property, damage to the environment, or a combination of these.

Concept note-3: -Risk assessments These include: work at height; hazardous substance (COSHH); manual handling; noise; vibration and lead.
