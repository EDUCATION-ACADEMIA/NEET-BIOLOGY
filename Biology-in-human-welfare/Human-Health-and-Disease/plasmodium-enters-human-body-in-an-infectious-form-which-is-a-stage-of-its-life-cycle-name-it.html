Plasmodium enters human body in an infectious form which is a stage of its life cycle. Name it.

(A) ** Sporozoites

(B) Gametocytes

(C) Merozoites

(D) Hypnozoites

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct answer is ‘Sporozoites’

Concept note-2: -Infective stage of Plasmodium to human beings is sporozoite and it is formed in gut of female Anopheles mosquito.

Concept note-3: -Plasmodium parasites have a complex life cycle that includes three stages namely Gametocytes, Sporozoites and Merozoites.

Concept note-4: -The sporozoites form of Plasmodium gains the entry into the human blood due to Anopheles biting. Sporozoites infect liver cells and mature into schizonts, which rupture and release merozoites which further attacks RBC and destroy them.

Concept note-5: -Solution : (i) Plasmodium enters the human body as sporozoites that are stored in the salivary glands of the female anopheles mosquito. When the mosquito bites a human, the sporozoites are introduced into his/her body.
