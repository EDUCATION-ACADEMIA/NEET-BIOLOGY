IMPROVEMENT IN FOOD PRODUCTION [SOURCES]

Increase in milk production is

(A) Green revolution

(B) ** White revolution

(C) yellow revolution

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The revolution associated with a sharp increase in milk production in the country is called the White Revolution in India also known as Operation Flood. White revolution period intended to make India a self-dependent nation in milk production.

Concept note-2: -The white revolution was initiated by Dr. Verghese Kurien in India in 1970. Its main motive was to make India self-dependent in milk production by improving milk production and the per capita availability of milk.

Concept note-3: -The revolution triggered a sharp increase in milk production in India. It was aptly called White Revolution or Operation Flood. Today, India is the world’s largest producer of milk. Dr Verghese Kurien helmed this transformative initiative and is known as the father of the White Revolution.

Concept note-4: -Flooding a dairy-deficient country with milk. In 1970, India set in motion the ‘White Revolution’, the world’s biggest dairy development program, led by Dr. Verghese Kurien. ‘Operation Flood’, as it is otherwise known, transformed the dairy-deficient nation into the global leader in milk production.

Concept note-5: -The white revolution is associated with an increase in the production of milk. In India, it was launched by Dr. Kureinn who is also known as the father of the white revolution.
