[SOURCES]
Blue revolution is related to

(A) Crop production

(B) ** Fish production

(C) Milk production

(D) Egg production

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Blue Revolution, with its multi-dimensional activities, focuses mainly on increasing fisheries production and productivity from aquaculture and fisheries resources, both inland and marine.

Concept note-2: -The Blue Revolution refers to the significant growth and intensification of global aquaculture production-domestication and farming of fish, shellfish, and aquatic plants-from the middle of the 20th century to present, particularly in underdeveloped countries.

Concept note-3: -Blue revolution is only associated with aquatic organisms; it has nothing to do with Milk production or Flower or Litmus. So the correct answer is option A. Fish.

Concept note-4: -The term blue revolution was introduced to increase the production of fishes. It gives more income to farmers and fishermen. iii. The process that involves the production of food without compromising with environmental quality is called sustainable agriculture.
