Which statement best describes a cell membrane?

(A) it is found only in animal cells

(B) it controls reproduction in the cell

(C) it is a cell

(D) ** it controls passage of materials into the cell

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (C) A flexible lipid double layer. The cell membrane is often referred to as a “fluid mosaic, ” a flexible structure containing phospholipids as well as some proteins and other important molecules.

Concept note-2: -The cell membrane is semipermeable (or selectively permeable). It is made of a phospholipid bilayer, along with other various lipids, proteins, and carbohydrates.

Concept note-3: -The cell membrane is a thin semi-permeable membrane that surrounds the cytoplasm of a cell. It is living in nature and mainly composed of lipids and proteins.

Concept note-4: -The cell membrane is said to be selectively permeable. Small molecules, for example, may pass through the membrane. If no energy is required for substances to pass through the membrane, the process is called passive transport.
