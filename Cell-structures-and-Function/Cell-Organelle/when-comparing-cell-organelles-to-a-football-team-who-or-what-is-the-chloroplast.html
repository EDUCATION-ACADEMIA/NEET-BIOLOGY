[SOURCES]
When comparing cell organelles to a football team, who or what is the chloroplast?

(A) ** offense

(B) coach

(C) cheerleader

(D) referee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The chloroplast is like the mascot. This is because the chloroplast stores up a lot of energy and uses it as food for plants, like the mascot stores up energy and uses it to pump up the players. The golgi apparatus is like the equipment manager of the football team.

Concept note-2: -The head coach is like the nucleus because the coach controls the team and the nucleus controls the cell. The bench is like the cytoplasm because the bench fills space just like the cytoplasm. The punter is like ER because the punter transports the ball down the field and the ER helps transport stuff.

Concept note-3: -Cell Analogy: A plant Cell–The Patriots Football Complex The Cell Membrane is like the stadium of a football team because the cell membrane protects the cell and controls what enters and leaves the cell just like the stadium holds everything inside of it and only lets in fans who want to watch in and out.

Concept note-4: -FOOTBALL STADIUM The nucleus of the atom would be the size of a pea in the centre of the stadium, and the electrons would be whizzing around the outer stands. Everything in between would be empty space.

Concept note-5: -The outside of the football stadium is like the cell membrane. The nucleus is the control center of the cell. The football field is where the action takes place, and controls the crowd’s reaction. The custodial workers clean up the trash and dispose of it, because it is no longer needed.
