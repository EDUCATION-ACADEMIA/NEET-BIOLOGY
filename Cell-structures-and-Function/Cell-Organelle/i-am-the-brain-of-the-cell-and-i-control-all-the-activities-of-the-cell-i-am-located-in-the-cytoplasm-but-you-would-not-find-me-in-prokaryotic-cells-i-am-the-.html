I am the “brain” of the cell and I control all the activities of the cell. I am located in the cytoplasm, but you would not find me in prokaryotic cells. I am the ____

(A) ** Nucleus

(B) Ribosomes

(C) Cell Membrane

(D) Mitochondria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -I am the brain of the cell, and i control all of the activities of the cell. I am located in the cytosol but you would not find me in prokaryotic cells. I am the ? I am the cells transport system.

Concept note-2: -The nucleus is like the remote control center of the cell. It acts as the cell’s brain by telling it what to do, how to grow, and when to reproduce. The nucleus is home to the cell’s genes. A membrane, a thin layer that allows chemicals to pass in and out to the rest of the cell, surrounds the nucleus.

Concept note-3: -By housing the cell’s genome, the nucleus serves both as the repository of genetic information and as the cell’s control center.

Concept note-4: -Nucleus. Known as the cell’s “command center, ” the nucleus is a large organelle that stores the cell’s DNA (deoxyribonucleic acid). The nucleus controls all of the cell’s activities, such as growth and metabolism, using the DNA’s genetic information.

Concept note-5: -The nucleus is the largest and most significant living organelle of the cell. All cell activities of the cell are regulated by the nucleus. Hence it is called brain or control centre of the cell. The division of nucleus during cell division is known as karyokinesis.
