CELL ORGANELLE

[SOURCES]
Which cell organelle has the function of taking in nutrients, breaking them down, and creating energy for the cell?

(A) nucleus

(B) ribsome

(C) endoplasmic reticulum

(D) ** mitochondria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mitochondria are known as the powerhouses of the cell. They are organelles that act like energy generators by taking in nutrients, breaking them down and creating energy rich molecules (ATP) for the cell.

Concept note-2: -The classic role of mitochondria is oxidative phosphorylation, which generates ATP by utilizing the energy released during the oxidation of the food we eat. ATP is used in turn as the primary energy source for most biochemical and physiological processes, such as growth, movement and homeostasis.

Concept note-3: -Mitochondria is an organelle in which the biochemical processes of respiration and energy production occur.

Concept note-4: -Mitochondria are complex organelles that convert energy from food into a form that the cell can use.

Concept note-5: -A lysosome is a membrane-bound cell organelle that contains digestive enzymes. Lysosomes are involved with various cell processes. They break down excess or worn-out cell parts.
