Select all of the statements that are true about mitosis.

(A) ** Mitosis produces diploid cells.

(B) Mitosis forms two unique cells.

(C) ** Mitosis includes four phases.

(D) ** Mitosis occurs only in body cells.

(E) Mitosis is used by prokaryotes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mitosis is a type of cell division that produces two identical daughter cells from a single diploid cell. Mitosis occurs in nearly every cell in the human body. It is essential for our body’s growth, development and repair.

Concept note-2: -So, the correct answer is ‘The cell divides twice’.

Concept note-3: -In mitosis, two daughter cells are formed by single division. It replaces and repairs cells. Number of chromosomes remains the same in mitosis.

Concept note-4: -So, the correct statement is ‘Mitosis takes place regularly in somatic cells, whereas meiosis occurs only in germ cells’.
