Which of the following phrases best describes cancer?

(A) absence of cyclins in the DNA

(B) multiple gene mutations on a chromosome of DNA

(C) ** uncontrolled cell growth caused by mutations in genes that control the cell cycle

(D) presence of genetic defects caused by hereditary disorders

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unlike normal genes, oncogenes cannot be turned off, so they cause uncontrolled cell growth. In normal cells, tumor suppressor genes prevent cancer by slowing or stopping cell growth. DNA changes that inactivate tumor suppressor genes can lead to uncontrolled cell growth and cancer.

Concept note-2: -Cancer is a disease in which some of the bodyâ€™s cells grow uncontrollably. There are many different types of cancer, and each begins when a single cell acquires a genomic change (or mutation) that allows the cell to divide and multiply unchecked.

Concept note-3: -Oncogenes. These turn a healthy cell into a cancerous cell. Mutations in these genes are not known to be inherited.

Concept note-4: -Unit 5.1: Definition of Cancer. The American Cancer Society (ACS) defines cancer as a group of diseases characterized by uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells. If the spread is not controlled, it can result in death.
