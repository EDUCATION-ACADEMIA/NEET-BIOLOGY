Which of the following is NOT a gamete (sex cell)?

(A) egg

(B) sperm

(C) ** skin cell

(D) all of the above are gametes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Somatic cells are a regular type of body cell that is not involved in any way in sexual reproduction. In humans, such cells are diploid and reproduce using the process of mitosis to create identical diploid copies of themselves when they split.

Concept note-2: -Gametes are an organismâ€™s reproductive cells. They are also referred to as sex cells. Female gametes are called ova or egg cells, and male gametes are called sperm. Gametes are haploid cells, and each cell carries only one copy of each chromosome.

Concept note-3: -Only the egg cell has a nucleus. Hint: A human skin cell is a type of somatic cell. Human egg cells are the female gamete cells. We know that somatic cells are diploid but gamete cells are not.

Concept note-4: -A somatic cell is a term for any cell except a sex cell. The sex cell (or gametes) is called the germline cell of the body. Somatic cells are present in internal organs, such as bone, blood, skin, and connective tissue.
