Physics (the study of matter, energy and motion) of the human body would relate more closely to the science of ____

(A) anatomy

(B) ** physiology

(C) Both anatomy and physiology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The laws of physics are used to explain several bodily functions including the mechanics of muscles and body movements, fluid mechanics of blood and air flow, hearing and acoustic properties of the ears, vision optics, heat and energy, acoustics, and electrical signaling.

Concept note-2: -Physiology is the study of life, specifically, how cells, tissues, and organisms function. Physiologists are constantly trying to answer key questions in areas ranging from the functions of single cells to the interactions between human populations and our environment here on earth, the moon, and beyond.

Concept note-3: -Physiology is the study of how the human body works. It describes the chemistry and physics behind basic body functions, from how molecules behave in cells to how systems of organs work together. It helps us understand what happens in a healthy body in everyday life and what goes wrong when someone gets sick.

Concept note-4: -Anatomy (from Ancient Greek (anatomḗ) ‘dissection’) is the branch of biology concerned with the study of the structure of organisms and their parts.
