Which is NOT a function for Lipids?

(A) Long term Energy Storage

(B) ** Chubify

(C) Insulation

(D) Cell Membrane Structure

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Number 1, lipids are composed of glycerol and fatty acid tails. Number 2, the oil belongs to the type of lipid biomolecule. Number 3, the option that is not a function of lipids is Chubify.

Concept note-2: -The answer is d. All lipids contain fatty acids. The statement that is not true is choice (d) All lipids contain fatty acids. Not all lipids contain fatty acid molecules.

Concept note-3: -3) Sterols are the least common type of lipid. Cholesterol is perhaps the best well-known sterol. Though cholesterol has a notorious reputation, the body gets only a small amount of its cholesterol through food-the body produces most of it.

Concept note-4: -Lipids play a very important role in our body. They are the structural component of the cell membrane. They help in providing energy and produce hormones in our body. They help in the proper digestion and absorption of food.
