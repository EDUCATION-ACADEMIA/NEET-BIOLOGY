What does hetero mean?

(A) same

(B) ** different

(C) inside

(D) outside

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -informal for heterosexual formal : sexually or romantically attracted to men if you are a woman, and women if you are a man: He’s gay? I thought he was hetero for sure. Synonym. straight (SEXUALITY) informal.

Concept note-2: -hetero-in American English combining form. a combining form meaning “different, ” “other, ” used in the formation of compound words.

Concept note-3: -Noun. hetro (plural hetros) (informal) A heterosexual person.

Concept note-4: -Hetero derives from the Greek word heteros meaning “different” or “other". It may refer to: Heterodoxy, belief or practice that differs from what is assumed as orthodoxy. Heterosexuality, attraction towards the opposite sex.
