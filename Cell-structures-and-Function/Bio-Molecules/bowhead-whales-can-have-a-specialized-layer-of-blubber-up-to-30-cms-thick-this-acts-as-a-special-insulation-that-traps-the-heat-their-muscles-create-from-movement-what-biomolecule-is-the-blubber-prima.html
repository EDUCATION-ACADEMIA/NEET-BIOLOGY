EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The energy stored in blubber includes both proteins (mostly collagen) and fats (mostly lipids).

Concept note-2: -Carbohydrates, in the form of starches and sugars, are the macronutrients required in the largest amounts. When eaten and broken down, carbohydrates provide the major source of energy to fuel our daily activities.

Concept note-3: -Albinism, caused by a deficiency of melanin pigment in the skin, hair, and eye (oculocutaneous albinism [OCA]), or primarily in the eye (ocular albinism [OA]), results from mutations in genes involved in the biosynthesis of melanin pigment.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: Enzymes are biological proteins.
