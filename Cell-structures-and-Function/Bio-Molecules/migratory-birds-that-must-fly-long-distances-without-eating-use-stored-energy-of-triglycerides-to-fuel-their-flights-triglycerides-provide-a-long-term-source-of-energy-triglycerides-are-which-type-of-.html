Migratory birds that must fly long distances without eating use stored energy of triglycerides to fuel their flights. Triglycerides provide a long-term source of energy. Triglycerides are which type of biomolecule?

(A) Nucleic Acid

(B) Protein

(C) ** Lipid

(D) Carbohydrate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nucleic Acids These molecules contain the genetic code, which has all the information necessary to build the body. The basic unit is called a nucleotide, which is composed of a sugar-phosphate backbone attached to one of four nitrogenous bases; cytosine, guanine, adenine or thymine.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The class of biomolecule that is mostly used as an energy storage molecule are polysaccharides. Specifically in some cells the storage molecule at the most basic level is glucose. In larger organisms the storage molecule for humans is glycogen which is used as a glucose storage.

Concept note-3: -There are four major classes of biological macromolecules (carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, and nucleic acids), and each is an important component of the cell and performs a wide array of functions. Combined, these molecules make up the majority of a cell’s mass.

Concept note-4: -Lipids are the main component of the cell membrane and are often referred to as fats. Proteins serve as pumps or channels moving materials in and out of the cell membrane. Carbohydrates are compounds that form sugars and are made of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen.
