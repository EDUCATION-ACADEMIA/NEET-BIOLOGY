A doctor advised a person to take injection of insulin because his:

(A) Blood pressure was high

(B) Heart beat was high

(C) ** Blood sugar was high

(D) Thyroxine level in blood was high

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A doctor advised a person to take an injection of insulin because his sugar level in blood was high. Insulin hormone decreases the level of glucose by promoting the cells to take up glucose and synthesize glycogen from it.

Concept note-2: -When youâ€™re not eating, your liver releases glucose so the body continually has energy. Long, ultra-long or intermediate-acting insulin helps the body use this glucose and keeps glucose levels from rising too high.

Concept note-3: -Diabetes is caused by high sugar level in blood. Diabetic patients are treated by giving injections of insulin as it lowers the blood sugar level by controlling the metabolism of sugar.

Concept note-4: -As blood sugar levels rise, the pancreas produces insulin, a hormone that prompts cells to absorb blood sugar for energy or storage. As cells absorb blood sugar, levels in the bloodstream begin to fall.
