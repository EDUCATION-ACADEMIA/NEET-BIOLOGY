(A) liver

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Liver secretes a juice known as bile. The function of the bile salts include, to make the food alkaline and to break down the fat molecules into smaller and simpler compounds. The process of breaking down fat into smaller globules is known as emulsification.

Concept note-2: -Bile is a liquid produced by your liver and stored in your gallbladder. When prompted by hormones and the vagus nerve, bile is released from your gallbladder into your duodenum and intestines. Your body then uses it to break down fats, absorb vitamins, and remove wastes that your body doesnâ€™t need.

Concept note-3: -Bile is a fluid that is made and released by the liver and stored in the gallbladder. Bile helps with digestion. It breaks down fats into fatty acids, which can be taken into the body by the digestive tract.
