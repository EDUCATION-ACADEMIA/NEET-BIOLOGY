BIOTECHNOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Biotechnology utilizes biomolecular and cellular processes to create new products and technologies that will improve our lives and stabilize Earths health. Which of the following biotechnologies has the ability to produce genetically identical organisms?

(A) Evolution

(B) Genetic engineering

(C) ** Cloning

(D) Artificial selection

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biotechnology is the use of genetic engineering applications to make a product or improve a process that will enhance human life. Biotechnology is the application of science, specifically biology, to life applications.

Concept note-2: -What is Biotechnology? Biotechnology is technology that utilizes biological systems, living organisms or parts of this to develop or create different products.

Concept note-3: -Recombinant DNA technology involves using enzymes and various laboratory techniques to manipulate and isolate DNA segments of interest. This method can be used to combine (or splice) DNA from different species or to create genes with new functions.
