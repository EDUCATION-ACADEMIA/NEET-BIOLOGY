Why could gene therapy be beneficial in medicinal practice?

(A) Gene therapy allows patients to seek advice for their genetic disorders

(B) ** Gene therapy treats the cause of disease

(C) Gene therapy is a simple process

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gene therapies can work by several mechanisms: Replacing a disease-causing gene with a healthy copy of the gene. Inactivating a disease-causing gene that is not functioning properly. Introducing a new or modified gene into the body to help treat a disease.

Concept note-2: -In the future, genetic therapies may be used to prevent, treat, or cure certain inherited disorders, such as cystic fibrosis, alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency, hemophilia, beta thalassemia, and sickle cell disease. They also may be used to treat cancers or infections, including HIV.

Concept note-3: -Gene therapy is a medical approach that treats or prevents disease by correcting the underlying genetic problem. Gene therapy techniques allow doctors to treat a disorder by altering a personâ€™s genetic makeup instead of using drugs or surgery.

Concept note-4: -Depending on what is wrong, scientists can do one of several things in gene therapy: They can replace a gene that is missing or is causing a problem. They can add genes to the body to help treat disease. Or they can turn off genes that are causing problems.
