Which describes a way scientists use biotechnology in the field of agriculture?

(A) DNA evidence is used to convict criminals

(B) ** Fruits and vegetables are altered to be more nutritious

(C) Vaccines are developed to help us live longer

(D) Bacteria are created to clean up oil spills

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Genetic Engineering of Plants. Perhaps the most direct way to use biotechnology to improve crop agriculture is to genetically engineer plants-that is, alter their basic genetic structure-so they have new characteristics that improve the efficiency of crop production.

Concept note-2: -Biotechnology provides farmers with tools that can make production cheaper and more manageable. For example, some biotechnology crops can be engineered to tolerate specific herbicides, which make weed control simpler and more efficient.

Concept note-3: -The benefits of biotechnology in agriculture are: It increases the crop yield. The pest control crops increase the protection of crops. The use of recombinant DNA technology in the crop increases the cropâ€™s nutritional value.

Concept note-4: -Biotechnology is a branch of science that develops or creates products using biological systems or components of them. Applications of biotechnology in the field of agriculture: Recombinant DNA technology is used to produce genetically modified products that produce a high yield in agriculture.

Concept note-5: -Plant biotechnology can be defined as the use of tissue culture and genetic engineering techniques to produce genetically modified plants that exhibit new or improved desirable characteristics.
