A food stain on a tablecloth is where meat cooked in oil has been split.It is washed in biological washing powder.What combination of enzymes should be present in the washing powder to remove the stain?

(A) amylase and lipase

(B) ** lipase and protease

(C) pectinase and amylase

(D) protease and pectinase

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(d) Forensic scientists often try to find DNA on items of stained clothing. The DNA can be used to identify individual people.

Concept note-2: -Enzymes help speed up chemical reactions in the human body. They are essential for respiration, digesting food, muscle and nerve function, among thousands of other roles.

Concept note-3: -Biological washing powders contain protease and lipase to remove protein stains and fat/grease from clothes. The enzymes break down proteins or fats on the fabric, forming water-soluble substances that can be washed away.

Concept note-4: -The warmer the environment, the faster the germination. The best average temperature to grow your plants is 18 to 24 째C (64 to 75 째F). Usually, it takes around 1 to 2 weeks to germinate.
