Assertion-E.coli having pBR322 with DNA insert at BamHI site cannot grow in medium containing tetracyclineReason-Recognition site for Bam HI is present in tetr region of pBRE322

(A) ** if both assertion and reason are true and reason is the correct explanation of assertion

(B) if both assertion and reason are true but reason is not the correct explanation of assertion

(C) if assertion is true but reason is false

(D) if both assertion and reason are false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Assertion: E. coli having pBR322 with DNA insert at BamHI site cannot grow in medium containing tetracycline. Reason: Recognition site for Bam HI is present in tetR region of pBR322. (a) Both assertion and reason are true, and the reason is the correct explanation of the assertion.

Concept note-2: -Recognition site for BamHI is present in tetR region of pBR322.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is ‘BamHl’.

Concept note-4: -If gene of interest is cloned at BamHI site in pBR322, the recombinant loses its resistance to tetracycline by insertional inactivation. Hence, the recombinant bacteria now would conclusively exhibit resistance to ampicillin.

Concept note-5: -So the correct option is ‘They are resistant of ampicillin but susceptible to tetracycline’
