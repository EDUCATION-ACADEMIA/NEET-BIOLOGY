In agriscience as well as other sciences, the method used to solve a problem is called the:

(A) educated-guess method.

(B) most-popular-decision technique.

(C) process of elimination.

(D) ** scientific method.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is meant by an agricultural education? Applied science uses the basic sciences in practical ways. Examples include entomology, agronomy, horticulture, and the animal sciences.

Concept note-2: -The science that studies plants is called Botany. All natural sciences, including botany, aim to understand how the natural world functions. To be able to do this we need to make observations, run experiments, generate and test hypotheses, record data, and report results.

Concept note-3: -Scientific Inquiry Two methods of logical thinking are used: inductive reasoning and deductive reasoning.

Concept note-4: -The first step of the scientific method is the “Question.” This step may also be referred to as the “Problem.” Your question should be worded so that it can be answered through experimentation. Keep your question concise and clear so that everyone knows what you are trying to solve.
