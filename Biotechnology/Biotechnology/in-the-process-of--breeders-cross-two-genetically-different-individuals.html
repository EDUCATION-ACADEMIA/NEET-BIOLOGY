[SOURCES]
In the process of ____, breeders cross two genetically different individuals

(A) inbreeding

(B) cloning

(C) PCR

(D) ** hybridization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hybridization is the process of crossing two parent varieties or breeds that are genetically different. It is highly frequent in plants and domestic animals to improve their quality and yield.

Concept note-2: -Hybridization is the mating of genetically differentiated individuals or groups and may involve crossing individuals within a species (also known as line crossing or strain crossing) or crossing individuals between separate species.

Concept note-3: -The cloning procedure doesn’t involve the breeding of two genetically different individuals. Therefore, the given statement is false.

Concept note-4: -Hybridization-producing new crop varieties-crossing two or more plants of unlike genetic constitution. Based on the nature & relationship of plants to be crossed, hybridization can be – Inter-varietal – Cross bet.

Concept note-5: -This crossing may be intervarietal (between different varieties), interspecific (between two different species of the same genus) or intergeneric (between different genera).
