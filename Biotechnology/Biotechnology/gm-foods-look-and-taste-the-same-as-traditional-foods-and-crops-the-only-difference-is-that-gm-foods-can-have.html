BIOTECHNOLOGY

[SOURCES]
GM foods look and taste the same as traditional foods and crops. The only difference is that GM foods can have

(A) Better shelf life

(B) Genes with traits that improve some characteristics of a plant.

(C) Addition nutrients

(D) ** All are possible

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Remember, conventional foods have never been “scientifically proven” to be safe, they are considered safe, purely based on their history of safe use. GM foods in contrast have to undergo formal, rigorous food safety assessments to ensure their safety.

Concept note-2: -The technology only affects the way the food is grown and has no impact on the taste, nutrition or safety of that food. So whether it’s GMO or non-GMO food, you get an equally safe and nutritious product.

Concept note-3: -Research shows that foods like eggs, dairy products, and meat that come from animals that eat GMO food are equal in nutritional value, safety, and quality to foods made from animals that eat only non-GMO food.

Concept note-4: -Genetic modification of plants involves adding a specific stretch of DNA into the plant’s genome, giving it new or different characteristics. This could include changing the way the plant grows, or making it resistant to a particular disease.
