BIOTECHNOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Someone who enjoys caring for animals might consider which biotechnology career?

(A) Research associate

(B) Laboratory assistant

(C) Greenhouse technician

(D) ** Veterinary technician

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What better way to contribute to the welfare of animals by treating and caring for them? If you are interested in medical science and are passionate about animals, then becoming a veterinary doctor is one of the best careers for animal lovers.

Concept note-2: -There is a wide array of scope for veterinary biotechnologists in numerous sectors such as research institutes, defense organizations, biotechnology companies, food-processing plants. Apart from research, they can opt for a teaching profession in different universities.

Concept note-3: -Biotechnology professionals often work in the medical industry, taking on roles in pharmaceutical manufacturing and medical equipment production. Graduates also work with the military in public and private sector positions. Read on to learn about five common industries that employ biotechnologists.

Concept note-4: -Medical biotechnology is defined as the application of biotechnology tools for producing medical products that can be used for the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases. The best-known products of medical biotechnology are antibiotics that are used to treat bacterial infections.

Concept note-5: -What is the Scope of Biotechnology? Being a combination of technology and science of biology, biotechnology has a broad scope to include sciences like health, virology, immunology, agriculture, seed technology, plant physiology, cell biology, etc.
