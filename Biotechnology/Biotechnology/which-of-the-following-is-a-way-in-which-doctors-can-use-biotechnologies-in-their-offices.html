(A) Input genes into a patient to grow taller or run faster.

(C) ** Doctors can predict if patients will develop a genetic disease.

(D) Doctors can give a patient new genes to pass on to their children.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gene therapy is a technique that modifies a personâ€™s genes to treat or cure disease. Gene therapies can work by several mechanisms: Replacing a disease-causing gene with a healthy copy of the gene.

Concept note-2: -Laboratory tests, including genetic testing: Molecular, chromosomal, and biochemical genetic or genomic testing are used to diagnose genetic disorders. Other laboratory tests that measure the levels of certain substances in blood and urine can also help suggest a diagnosis.

Concept note-3: -One way that genetic engineering is already being used to treat disease in humans. Scientist inject a corrected gene into a person suffering from a disease. If the gene is taken up by the diseased body cell, the proper protein can be produced, reversing the disease.
