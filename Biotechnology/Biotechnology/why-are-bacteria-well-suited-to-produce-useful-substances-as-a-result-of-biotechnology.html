Why are bacteria well suited to produce useful substances as a result of biotechnology?

(A) They are prokaryotes

(B) ** They reproduce rapidly

(C) They are not harmful to humans

(D) They are resistant to antibiotics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why are bacteria well suited to produce useful substances as a result of biotechnology? They are prokaryotes. They cannot produce substances that are harmful to humans. They are resistant to antibiotics.

Concept note-2: -Bacteria in molecular biology. Bacteria that reproduce quickly and are easy to grow in the lab make good model organisms for many scientific studies.

Concept note-3: -The two microbes which have a major contribution in the field of biotechnology are E. coli and Lactobacillus. E. coli is generally used for protein production and genetic transformation.

Concept note-4: -GM bacteria are used in the production of enzymes such as milk-clotting enzymes for cheese production and food/feed additives such as aspartame and l-lysine.
