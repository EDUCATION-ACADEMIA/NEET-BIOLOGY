Choose the correct statement about the diffusion rate of a substance.

(A) The particles will diffuse faster when they are far away from each other.

(B) ** A Greater surface area equals greater diffusion rate.

(C) Lower temperature equals higher diffusion rate.

(D) The size of molecules have no impact on diffusion rate.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The surface area of the membrane affects the rate of diffusion. As the surface area of the membrane increases, the rate of diffusion also increases, as there is more space for molecules to diffuse across the membrane. Distance. The shorter the distance the substances have to move, the faster the rate of diffusion.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is 3 i.e. The rate of diffusion increases on increasing with the temperature of the diffusing substance.

Concept note-3: -The larger the surface area, the faster the rate of diffusion.

Concept note-4: -The larger the surface area to volume ratio, the quicker the rate of diffusion takes place. The substance simply has more area to diffuse across, despite being the same volume.
