[SOURCES]
Rubber also one type of

(A) Alkaloids

(B) Tannins Tannins

(C) Resins

(D) Gums

(E) ** Latex Latex

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The simplest answer: latex is rubber. Typically, it is composed of about fifty-five percent water and around forty percent rubber material. Latex is the soft white substance found beneath the bark of a mature rubber tree .

Concept note-2: -Many people think latex is another word for rubber. But latex and rubber are not the same thing. Latex is a milky white substance that may be used in production as is or collected, treated, and processed to become rubber.

Concept note-3: -There are two primary types of rubber, namely natural rubber and synthetic rubber.

Concept note-4: -Not all types of latex are from natural rubber and contain the proteins responsible for natural rubber latex allergy. For example, products containing nitrile and polyvinyl chloride are made of synthetic latex that does not contain those proteins and will not cause a latex allergy.

Concept note-5: -So does rubber contain latex? It certainly does, as natural rubber is made from latex.
