These substances are swelling by absorbing water

(A) Alkaloids

(B) Tannins Tannins

(C) Resins

(D) ** Gums are glues

(E) Latex Latex

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In this exercise, gum arabic from the Acacia plant was used to make an edible glue.

Concept note-2: -Clear Gum Glue is perfect for a wide range of art and craft activities. It adheres to most surfaces and dries clear so it won’t get in the way of your artwork. This glue adheres to materials including paper, cardboard and textiles. It dries clear and smooth so it won’t detract from your art.

Concept note-3: -Mucilages, when mixed with water have historically been used as a glue, especially for bonding paper items such as postage stamps and envelope flaps.

Concept note-4: -Natural gums are long chains of sugars (polysaccharides) within native plant materials that are either water-soluble or capable of absorbing water. Gels are formed when these polysaccharides are mixed with water. Gums are only partially digested by humans and typically have few adverse side effects.
