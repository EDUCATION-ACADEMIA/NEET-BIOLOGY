Which is a leading cause of death in China?

(A) skin cancer

(B) ** respiratory and heart disease

(C) injuries from automobile accidents

(D) injuries related to industrial and factory accidents

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is the leading cause of death (COD) in China.

Concept note-2: -The top cause of death and disability in 2019 is Stroke, of type Non-communicable diseases, which has increased by 9.53 percent since 2009. The axis shows the percent change from-30 percent to 30 percent.

Concept note-3: -This study reveals that in China, the top 5 consistent causes of death are malignant tumor, cerebrovascular disease, heart trouble, respiratory disease, and trauma and toxicosis during the past 18 years.

Concept note-4: -The biggest cause of death in China is strokes, followed by heart disease, chronic pulmonary disease and then lung cancer, according to the global burden of disease study produced by the US Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation. Smoking is a contributing factor in many of those cases.

Concept note-5: -Introduction. China is facing a cardiovascular disease (CVD) crisis. In China, more than 40% of deaths are attributable to CVD [1], and the total number of CVD deaths in China is now larger than any other country around the world [2].
