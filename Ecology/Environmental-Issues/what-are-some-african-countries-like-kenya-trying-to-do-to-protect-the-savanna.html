What are some African countries, like Kenya, trying to do to protect the savanna?

(A) ** Setting land aside as national parks

(B) Tariffs against countries who cut trees.

(C) Stopping all commercial logging in central Africa.

(D) Refusing to let people move into the savanna area

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Savanna restoration techniques therefore include the reintroduction of natural disturbances such as the use of prescribed burns, grazing management, reintroduction of herbivores, but also the elimination of invasive species.

Concept note-2: -Why are most game parks found in the savannah grasslands? There is plenty of pasture for the animals. There is a complete food chain for the animals. It is a habitat (home) for wild animals.

Concept note-3: -It covers Guinea, Sierra Leone, Liberia, Cote D’ivore, Ghana, Togo, Benin, Nigeria, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Chad, Sudan, Ethiopia, Somalia, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Angola, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, Kenya, Tanzania, Malawi, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Botswana, and South Africa.

Concept note-4: -About 8% of the Kenya’s land mass is protected area for wildlife conservation.
