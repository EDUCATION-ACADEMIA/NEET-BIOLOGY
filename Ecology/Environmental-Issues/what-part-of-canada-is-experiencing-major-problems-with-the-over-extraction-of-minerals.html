What part of Canada is experiencing major problems with the over-extraction of minerals?

(A) The Great Lakes

(B) ** Canadian Shield

(C) Eastern Canada

(D) St. Lawrence Seaway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Southern Canada (near the Great Lakes region) experiences the highest levels of acid rain.

Concept note-2: -Because the soil in the land is very rich with all these types of minerals, miners have taken advantage of this by extensively digging and mining the area which has caused the area of land with these minerals to be ruined and unsalvageable.

Concept note-3: -The primary environmental disaster in the Canadian Shield is deforestation. Since the Shield is engulfed with immense forests, it enchants many industries dealing with timber. Due to the high number of trees, the rate of cutting them down is high for making furniture, paper, and many others.

Concept note-4: -Some of the resources that the Canadian Shield gives to Canadians are: 1) Furs-from fur bearing animals (hunting and trapping is still a large industry). 2) Minerals-this makes a lot of sense because the Shield is solid rock. Canadians extract copper, gold, nickel, zinc and lead from this area.

Concept note-5: -Mines often contaminate the soil, contaminate water and disrupt the local ecosystem.
