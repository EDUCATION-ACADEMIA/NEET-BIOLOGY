Which of the following is not a condition of sustainability?

(A) considering ways of not being harmful to the environment

(B) avoiding depleting natural resources

(C) ** using resources without regard to the effects on the environment

(D) supporting long-term ecological balance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The term sustainability is broadly used to indicate programs, initiatives and actions aimed at the preservation of a particular resource. However, it actually refers to four distinct areas: human, social, economic and environmental – known as the four pillars of sustainability.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Nuclear Power.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Afforestation.

Concept note-4: -The figure at the top of this page suggests that there are three pillars of sustainability – economic viability, environmental protection and social equity.
