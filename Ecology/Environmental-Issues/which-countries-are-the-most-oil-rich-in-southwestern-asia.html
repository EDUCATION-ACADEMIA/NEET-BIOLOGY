Which countries are the most oil-rich in Southwestern Asia?

(A) ** Iran, Iraq, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia

(B) Jordan, Israel, Syria, Lebanon

(C) Iraq, Kuwait, Israel, Lebanon

(D) Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Southwest Asian countries with the greatest reserves of natural gas and oil are Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Iran, and Kuwait. Some other countries have smaller reserves, especially those found around the Arabian Gulf.

Concept note-2: -Southwest Asia has the world’s largest known oil reserves, and Saudi Arabia and Iran have the largest deposits of oil.

Concept note-3: -Iraq has increased production since the end of the Iraq War and is now the second-largest producer in the Middle East. Iran is one of the world’s largest oil producer, but output is below potential due to economic sanctions.

Concept note-4: -What are the oil countries of Southwest Asia? Syria, Iraq, Iran, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait and U.A.E.
