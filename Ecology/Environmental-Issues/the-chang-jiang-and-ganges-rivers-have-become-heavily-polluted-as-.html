The Chang Jiang and Ganges Rivers have become heavily polluted as ____

(A) ** industrial, agricultural, and domestic waste have been dumped into them

(B) industrial waste has been dumped into them

(C) agricultural waste has been dumped into them

(D) domestic waste has been dumped into them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The answer is yes. Industrial wastewater discharge, agricultural chemical fertilizer, sediment accumulation, ship garbage and acid rain were the main Yangtze River pollution causes.

Concept note-2: -Why is the pollution of the Chang Jiang a significant problem in China? The river is the main source of drinking water in China. The river is the main source of hydropower in China.

Concept note-3: -What is one effect of the pollution of the Chang Jiang (Yangtze)? Many Chinese citizens lack access to safe drinking water.

Concept note-4: -Since ancient times, many people (now living in densely populated areas) have relied on this river for their daily needs. As a result of the industrial revolution, the Ganga River has worked its way up to being one of the world’s most polluted rivers.
