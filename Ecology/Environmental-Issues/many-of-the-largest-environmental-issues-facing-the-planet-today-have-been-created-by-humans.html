ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES

[SOURCES]
Many of the largest environmental issues facing the planet today have been created by humans.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Climate change is the big environmental problem that humanity will face over the next decade, but it isn’t the only one. We’ll take a look at some of them-from water shortages and loss of biodiversity to waste management-and discuss the challenges we have ahead of us.

Concept note-2: -Humans impact the physical environment in many ways: overpopulation, pollution, burning fossil fuels, and deforestation. Changes like these have triggered climate change, soil erosion, poor air quality, and undrinkable water.

Concept note-3: -Impacts from human activity on land and in the water can influence ecosystems profoundly. Climate change, ocean acidification, permafrost melting, habitat loss, eutrophication, stormwater runoff, air pollution, contaminants, and invasive species are among many problems facing ecosystems.
