What man made structure was built in Central America in 1914 to create a water way from the Pacific Ocean to the Atlantic Ocean?

(A) ** Panama Canal

(B) Bering Strait

(C) Golden Gate Bridge

(D) Colombia-Venezuela Bridge

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -President Theodore Roosevelt oversaw the realization of a long-term United States goal-a trans-isthmian canal.

Concept note-2: -In 1914, the Panama Canal opens, after ten years of construction, connecting the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans.

Concept note-3: -Theodore Roosevelt, negotiated the Hay–Bunau-Varilla Treaty, giving the U.S. control of the Canal Zone. Work under U.S. supervision began in 1904, and the Panama Canal was completed in 1914. Tens of thousands of people, mostly labourers from Barbados, Martinique, and Guadeloupe, worked on the project.

Concept note-4: -The Panama Canal is a constructed waterway that connects the Atlantic and Pacific oceans across the Isthmus of Panama.
