BIODIVERSITY AND ITS CONSERVATION

[SOURCES]
Variety of species in a given area refers to what?

(A) Ecosystem diversity

(B) ** Species diversity

(C) Genetic Diversity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biodiversity refers to the variety of living organisms within a given area.

Concept note-2: -Species Diversity is simply the number and relative abundance of species found in a given biological organisation (population, ecosystem, Earth). Species are the basic units of biological classification and hence, this is the measure most commonly associated with the term ‘biodiversity’.

Concept note-3: -SPECIES BIODIVERSITY: This refers to the variety of species occupying a particular area.

Concept note-4: -Usually three levels of biodiversity are discussed-genetic, species, and ecosystem diversity.

Concept note-5: -Species diversity is determined not only by the number of species within a biological community-i.e., species richness-but also by the relative abundance of individuals in that community.
