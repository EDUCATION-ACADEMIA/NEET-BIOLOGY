BIODIVERSITY AND ITS CONSERVATION

[SOURCES]
I can have an impact on the local biodiversity by ____ (many answers)

(A) ** Growing a diverse garden

(B) ** Voting for biodiversity projects in my community

(C) ** Preserving/creating habitat for local wildlife in my backyard or porch.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Support local and regional projects aimed at tackling biodiversity loss. Buying fewer products and making sure the products you do buy minimise the impact on biodiversity. Investing in ways that promote biodiversity. Reducing waste of consumer goods: food, clothes, electrical appliances, etc.

Concept note-2: -Plant local flowers, fruits and vegetables. They should also be able to tell you where they source their plants from-the more local, the better. By supporting local wildlife, youâ€™re helping to preserve the biodiversity of your area and support its local ecosystem.

Concept note-3: -Biodiversity supports human and societal needs, including food and nutrition security, energy, development of medicines and pharmaceuticals and freshwater, which together underpin good health. It also supports economic opportunities, and leisure activities that contribute to overall wellbeing.

Concept note-4: -Leave critical wildlife habitat undisturbed, especially nesting and denning sites. Promote wildlife use by setting up bird and bat houses. Eradicate and control introduced weeds on your property. Keep vehicles on main roads to reduce the spread of weeds and disturbance to wildlife.
