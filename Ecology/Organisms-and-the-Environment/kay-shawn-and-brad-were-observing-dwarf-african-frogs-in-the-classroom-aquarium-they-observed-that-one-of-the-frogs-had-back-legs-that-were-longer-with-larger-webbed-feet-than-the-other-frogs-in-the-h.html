Concept note-2: -The given statement is true. Frogs have strong back legs to jump on land and webbed feet to swim in water.

Concept note-3: -Skin that prevents loss of water. Eyelids that allow them to adapt to vision outside of the water. An eardrum developed to separate the external ear from the middle ear.
