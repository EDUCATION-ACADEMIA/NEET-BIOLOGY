ORGANISMS AND THE ENVIRONMENT

[SOURCES]
A flower bed is created but was left bare over the summer. What types of plants might you see at the start of school?

(A) nothing

(B) ** weeds

(C) shrubs (bushes)

(D) Trees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The number of organisms an ecosystem can support depends on the resources available and abiotic factors, such as quantity of light and water, range of temperatures, and soil composition.

Concept note-2: -Low biodiversity is when there are a few prominent species and a low number of other species within the habitat. High biodiversity is a habitat or ecosystem that has a high number of different species.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following is an example of one stage in ecological succession? A tree falls over in a forest.

Concept note-4: -Answer. Answer: Biodiversity prevents habitats from being destroyed by people. Maintaining diversity allows species to use up the resources in an ecosystem.
