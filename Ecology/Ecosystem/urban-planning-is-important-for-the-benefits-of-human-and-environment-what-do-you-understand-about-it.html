Urban planning is important for the benefits of human and environment. What do you understand about it?

(A) ** The development of an area by maintaining some green areas

(B) The development of an area by destroying green areas

(C) The development of an area without control

(D) The development of an area by cutting down trees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Urban green space: benefits to health Green spaces improve air quality, reduce noise and enhance biodiversity (Maes et al., 2019). Green spaces also moderate temperatures during hot periods and provide cool and shaded areas (Romanello et al., 2021).

Concept note-2: -The immediate ecological benefits of green spaces are reduction in the urban heat island effect, cleaner air, and reduced noise pollution, which have been shown to improve health-related quality of life, and reduce both morbidity and mortality.

Concept note-3: -Urban planning encompasses the preparation of plans for and the regulation and management of towns, cities, and metropolitan regions. It attempts to organize sociospatial relations across different scales of government and governance.

Concept note-4: -With a thoroughly planned layout, a city can provide its residents with access to all essential services, points of interest, and amenities. At the same time, the unfavourable aspects of urban life are reduced, leading to an overall healthier lifestyle and improved quality of life.
