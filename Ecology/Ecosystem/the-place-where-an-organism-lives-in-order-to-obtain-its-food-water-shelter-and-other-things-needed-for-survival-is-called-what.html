The place where an organism lives in order to obtain its food, water, shelter and other things needed for survival is called what?

(A) Niche

(B) ** Habitat

(C) Home

(D) Ecosystem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A habitat is an environment where an organism lives throughout the year or for shorter periods of time to find a mate. The habitat contains all an animal needs to survive such as food and shelter. A microhabitat is a small area which differs somehow from the surrounding habitat.

Concept note-2: -The place where an organism lives and that provides the things the organism needs is called its habitat. Needs include food, water, and shelter. A single ecosystem may contain many habitats.

Concept note-3: -A habitat is a place or an environment where an organism lives.

Concept note-4: -Habitat – place where an organism lives; provides the food, shelter, moisture, temperature, and other factors required for the organism’s survival.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Solution: The surroundings where plants and animals live is called their habitat. For example, the habitat of a frog is fresh water, while the habitat of a camel is a desert.
