Heterotrophs that prey on other heterotrophs, such as wolves, lions, and lynxes are called

(A) ** carnivore

(B) herbivore

(C) omnivore

(D) decomposer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Heterotrophs that prey on other heterotrophs, such as wolves, lions, and lynxes, shown in Figure 1, are called carnivores (KAR nuh vorz). In addition to herbivores and carnivores, there are organisms that eat both plants and animals, called omnivores (AHM nih vorz).
