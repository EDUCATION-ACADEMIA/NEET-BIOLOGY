ECOSYSTEM

[SOURCES]
In a forest ecosystem, a food chain might look like this:green plants → rabbit → fox → panther If the green plants are destroyed in a forest fire, which short-term effect will this have on the organisms in this food chain?

(A) ** There would be fewer rabbits for the foxes to eat.

(B) The panthers would have to begin to eat green plants.

(C) There would be an increase in the fox population

(D) There would be more green plants for the rabbits to eat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Each living thing is a part of multiple food chains – for example, grass is a part of the food chain: grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → eagle, and also of the food chain: grass → deer → tiger.

Concept note-2: -There are two types of food chains: the grazing food chain, beginning with autotrophs, and the detrital food chain, beginning with dead organic matter (Smith & Smith 2009).

Concept note-3: -In the forest’s ecosystem, the trees, shrubs and moss are all producers. They turn water and sunlight into the energy they need to live and grow, through a process called photosynthesis.

Concept note-4: -All organisms in an ecosystem depend on each other. Food chains show the flow of energy from one organism to another. Food chains show the feeding relationships between organisms. Food webs show how all the food chains in an ecosystem interact.
