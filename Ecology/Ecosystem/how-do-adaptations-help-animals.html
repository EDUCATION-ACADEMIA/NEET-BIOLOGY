How do adaptations help animals?

(A) To escape animals

(B) to hunt for food

(C) to survive in their habitats

(D) ** All of these are correct

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adaptation can protect animals from predators or from harsh weather. Many birds can hide in the tall grass and weeds and insects can change their colour to blend into the surroundings. This makes it difficult for predators to seek them out for food.

Concept note-2: -All organisms have adaptations that help them survive and thrive. Some adaptations are structural. Structural adaptations are physical features of an organism like the bill on a bird or the fur on a bear. Other adaptations are behavioral.

Concept note-3: -There are three types of adaptations: structural, physiological, and behavioral. Structural adaptations are how the animalâ€™s body functions or looks on the outside. Body parts (like feet and ears) and body coverings (like fur and scales) are structural adaptations.
