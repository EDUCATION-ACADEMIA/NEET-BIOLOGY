KINGDOM PROTISTA

[SOURCES]
I have a nucleus, can perform photosynthesis and am multicellular. What kingdom do I belong to?

(A) Animalia

(B) Archaebacteria

(C) ** Plantae

(D) Protist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Plantae is the kingdom of plants. Important characteristics of plants are: 1. Plants are multicellular.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The kingdom that a multicellular, eukaryotic, photosynthetic organism belongs to is the kingdom Plantae. All plants are in this kingdom and have some common characteristics.

Concept note-3: -Animals are multicellular, and move with the aid of cilia, flagella, or muscular organs based on contractile proteins. They have organelles including a nucleus, but no chloroplasts or cell walls. Animals acquire nutrients by ingestion.

Concept note-4: -The animal kingdom is the largest kingdom with over 1 million known species. All animals consist of many complex cells. They are also heterotrophs.

Concept note-5: -Organisms belonging to the plant kingdom are eukaryotic and autotrophic. They synthesise their own food by photosynthesis.
