Listed below are the characteristics of an organism.· Unicellular, oval shape with pointed anterior· Green in colour· Has a cup-shaped chloroplast containing pyrenoidThe organism belongs to phylum ____

(A) Euglenophyta

(B) Phaeophyta

(C) ** Chlorophyta

(D) Rhizopoda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Genus of Chlamydomonas has a single large cup-shaped chloroplast into which pyrenoids like structure are present.

Concept note-2: -Unicellular organism with chlorophyll a and b and possessing cup-shaped chloroplast is Chlamydomonas.

Concept note-3: -Complete answer :A cup shaped chloroplast is present in Chlamydomonas .

Concept note-4: -The chloroplast of spirogyra is long, spirally coiled ribbon shaped with pyrenoids.
