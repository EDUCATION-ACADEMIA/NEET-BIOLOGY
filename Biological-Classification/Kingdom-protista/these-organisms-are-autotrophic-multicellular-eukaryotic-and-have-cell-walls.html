BIOLOGICAL CLASSIFICATION

KINGDOM PROTISTA

[SOURCES]
These organisms are autotrophic, multicellular, eukaryotic, and have cell walls.

(A) Animal

(B) ** Plant

(C) Fungi

(D) Protist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All multicellular eukaryotic organisms are classified as members of the kingdom Plantae. They are autotrophs because they use solar energy to produce their food. Plants have cellulose as a cell wall material.

Concept note-2: -All plants are multicellular organisms made of Eukaryotic cells that have a cell wall. They get food through photosynthesis so they are autotrophs.

Concept note-3: -In which kingdom multicellular eukaryotic autotrophic organisms are included? Solution : In Plantae kingdom, multicellular eukaryotic autotrophic organisms are included.

Concept note-4: -Plant Kingdom – Plantae Kingdom Plantae includes all the plants. They are eukaryotic, multicellular and autotrophic organisms. The plant cell contains a rigid cell wall.

Concept note-5: -Kingdom fungi are eukaryotic, multicellular organisms having cells with chitinous cell wall and show heterotrophic mode of nutrition.
