BIOLOGICAL CLASSIFICATION
VIROIDS [SOURCES]

Virus families end in the suffix

(A) -viroid

(B) -viri

(C) ** -viridae

(D) -virion

(E) viriviri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The highest taxonomic group among viruses is the family; families are named with a suffix-viridae. Subfamilies have the suffix-virinae; genera the suffix-virus.

Concept note-2: -Main rules for the correct spelling of names of viruses and related agents: The name of the order always ends in the suffix –virales and is written in italics and the first letter is capitalized. The name of the family always ends in the suffix –viridae and is written in italics and the first letter is capitalized.

Concept note-3: -The first broad division used in virus classification separates viruses into vertebrate viruses, invertebrate viruses, plant viruses, bacterial viruses, and algae, fungi, yeast, and protozoan viruses.
