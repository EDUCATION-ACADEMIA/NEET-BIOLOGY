[SOURCES]
I am something that is alive or was once alive ____

(A) Abiotic Factors

(B) ** Biotic Factors

(C) Bionic Flavors

(D) Biometric Robots

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biotic factors are, in entirety, anything that affects a living organism that is itself alive. Such things include animals which consume the organism in question, or the food that the organism consumes.

Concept note-2: -Biotic factors are living or once-living organisms in the ecosystem. These are obtained from the biosphere and are capable of reproduction. Examples of biotic factors are animals, birds, plants, fungi, and other similar organisms.

Concept note-3: -A: Biotic, because it was once a living thing. Things that are abiotic factors were never living.

Concept note-4: -Biotic factors include animals, plants, fungi, bacteria, and protists.

Concept note-5: -Biotic factors include not only living things but interactions between organisms, such as predation, parasitism, and competition. Abiotic factors include non-living components, as well as chemical and physical factors, that living organisms require in order to thrive.
