Which of the following best describes a gene?

(A) ** A section of DNA

(B) A bundle of DNA

(C) Every cell in your body has 23 pairs of genes

(D) All of the genetic material in your cells

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A gene is a segment of DNA with a particular sequence of nucleotides.

Concept note-2: -Correct Option-B A specific length of DNA responsible for the inheritance and expression of the character. Explanation-Gene is a particular segment of DNA which is responsible for the inheritance and expression of a particular character.

Concept note-3: -The answer is nucleic acid sequence.

Concept note-4: -A gene is the basic physical and functional unit of heredity. Genes are made up of DNA. Some genes act as instructions to make molecules called proteins.

Concept note-5: -Each DNA sequence that contains instructions to make a protein is known as a gene. The size of a gene may vary greatly, ranging from about 1, 000 bases to 1 million bases in humans. Genes only make up about 1 percent of the DNA sequence.
