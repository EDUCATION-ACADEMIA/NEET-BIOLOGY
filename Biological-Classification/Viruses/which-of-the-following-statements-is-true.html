Which of the following statements is true?

(A) ** Prokaryotic cells came before Eukaryotic cells

(B) Eukaryotic cells don’t have a nucleus and Prokaryotic cells do

(C) Eukaryotic cells are simple and Prokaryotic cells are complex

(D) Prokaryotic cells are bigger than Eukaryotic cells

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So the correct option is ‘prokaryotes evolved before eukaryotes’.

Concept note-2: -Prokaryotic cells were some of the earliest life forms on earth. They first appear in the fossil record around 4 billion years ago. Prokaryotes were around for a long, long time before eukaryotic cells appeared around 1.8 billion years ago.

Concept note-3: -According to the endosymbiotic theory, the first eukaryotic cells evolved from a symbiotic relationship between two or more prokaryotic cells. Smaller prokaryotic cells were engulfed by (or invaded) larger prokaryotic cells.

Concept note-4: -Final answer: They have a well-organized nucleus and they have a nuclear membrane statements are true for eukaryotic cells.

Concept note-5: -Prokaryotic cells do not have a well defined nucleus. Their genetic material is called a nucleoid. Membrane bound cell organelles are absent. They are surrounded by a cell wall.
