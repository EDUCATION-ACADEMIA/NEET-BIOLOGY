Which of these diseases are caused by bacteria (check 2)?

(A) COVID-19

(B) ** Lyme Disease

(C) ** Rabies

(D) Ringworm

(E) Malaria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rabies is a preventable viral disease most often transmitted through the bite of a rabid animal. The rabies virus infects the central nervous system of mammals, ultimately causing disease in the brain and death.

Concept note-2: -Rabies is a fatal but preventable viral disease. It can spread to people and pets if they are bitten or scratched by a rabid animal.

Concept note-3: -The rabies virus causes a rabies infection. The virus spreads through the saliva of infected animals. Infected animals can spread the virus by biting another animal or a person. In rare cases, rabies can be spread when infected saliva gets into an open wound or the mucous membranes, such as the mouth or eyes.

Concept note-4: -Rabies is a viral disease that is spread through the animal bite such as the dog. It is caused by the infection of rabies virus. The infection caused by this leads to encephalomyelitis i.e the inflammation of the brain as well as the spinal cord.
