An area in Kulonprogo, one hectare of land is planted with citrus fruit trees. Another hectare of land is planted with lime trees. To know and make it easier to harvest and sell, it is necessary to classify it. So, the classification is ____

(A) Grouping of living things based on common characteristics

(B) Grouping of living things based on differences in characteristics

(C) ** Grouping of living things based on similarities and differences in characteristics

(D) Grouping living things by color

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A typical tree spacing is 6m between rows by 3m between trees, meaning that 555 trees per hectare are planted.

Concept note-2: -Normal spacing – 4.5 m x 4.5 m; Plant population – 494/ha. In very light soils, the spacing may be 4 m x 4 m. In the fertile soil, and in high rainfall areas spacing may be 5 m x 5 m.

Concept note-3: -Orchard is an area of land where fruit trees are grown.

Concept note-4: -Citrus trees do best in temperatures between 55 and 90 degrees Fahrenheit. Ideal temperatures for the spring and summer range from 75 to 90 degrees. During fall and winter, the best temperature range for growing citrus is 60 to 70 degrees. Containerized citrus trees can be moved indoors during harsh cold weather.
