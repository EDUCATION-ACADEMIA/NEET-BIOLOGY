KINGDOM ANIMALS

[SOURCES]
Which kingdom has animals that lays eggs and has feathers?

(A) ** Birds

(B) Fish

(C) Mammals

(D) Amphibians

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Birds belong to the class Aves and posses the characteristic feature to lay eggs. Although, there are some organisms which are not birds but can lay eggs. Amphibians, snakes, and fish lay eggs. Reptiles including lizards, turtle, crocodile etc.

Concept note-2: -Birds are warm blooded and lay eggs. Most have feathers and can fly.

Concept note-3: -The platypus (Ornithorhynchus anatinus) has a puzzling array of features. Not only does it have that iconic duck bill, it lays eggs like a bird or reptile but feeds milk to its young like a mammal.

Concept note-4: -Oviparous animals are those that lay eggs, with little or no other embryonic development within the mother. This is the reproductive method of most fish, amphibians, reptiles, all birds, and the monotremes.

Concept note-5: -Egg Laying Animals Many of the animals reproduce by laying eggs. The eggs are laid in a safe place like nest, borrows, holes etc.
