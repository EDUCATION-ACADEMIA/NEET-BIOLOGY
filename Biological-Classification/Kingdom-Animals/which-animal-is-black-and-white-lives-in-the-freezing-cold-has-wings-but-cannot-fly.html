Which animal is black and white, lives in the freezing cold, has wings but cannot fly?

(A) zebra

(B) polar bear

(C) ** penguin

(D) wolf

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer :-Penguin. Penguins are found mainly in black and white color . Penguins have wings but they cannot fly .

Concept note-2: -It may seem strange that among the more than 10, 000 bird species in the world today is a group that literally cannot fly or sing, and whose wings are more fluff than feather. These are the ratites: the ostrich, emu, rhea, kiwi and cassowary.

Concept note-3: -No list of flightless birds would be complete without the penguin. All 18 species of penguin are unable to fly, and are in fact better built for swimming and diving, which they spend the majority of their time doing. Their short legs and stocky build give them a distinctive waddling walk.

Concept note-4: -A penguin is a type of large black and white sea bird found mainly in the Antarctic. Penguins cannot fly but use their short wings for swimming.
