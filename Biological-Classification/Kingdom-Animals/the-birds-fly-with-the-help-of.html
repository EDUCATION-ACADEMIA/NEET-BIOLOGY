The birds fly with the help of

(A) Feathers

(B) Tail

(C) Beak

(D) ** Wings

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The wings of birds have specialized feathers for flying called “flight feathers.” The power for flight is generated by repetitively pushing the flight feathers downward.

Concept note-2: -Feathers are the magic material that cover the bodies of all birds and help them to fly.

Concept note-3: -Birds fly by flapping their wings. Flight involves moving upward, against the force of gravity, and forward too. The power for this comes when the massive chest muscles pull the wings down. These muscles are 10 times bigger than the muscles that pull the wings back up.

Concept note-4: -The birds fly with the help of wings.
