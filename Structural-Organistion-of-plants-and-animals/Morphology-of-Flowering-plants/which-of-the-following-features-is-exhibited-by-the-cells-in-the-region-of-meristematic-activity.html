Which of the following features is exhibited by the cells in the region of meristematic activity?

(A) Large size

(B) Thick cell walls

(C) ** Dense protoplasm

(D) Low rate of cell division

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct answer is ‘Thin wall, dense protoplasm, no intercellular spaces, isodiametric and nucleate’.

Concept note-2: -Meristematic cells of the plants are the actively dividing part of the plant. They ar thin walled, densly protoplasmic, isodiametric and possess a large nucleus.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Cell active with dense cytoplasm, thin cellulose walls, and prominent nuclei. Plant Meristematic tissues are active cells or groups of active cells that have the ability to divide.

Concept note-4: -Meristematic cells have immense potential to divide. For this purpose, they have dense cytoplasm and thin cell wall. Vacuoles possess cell sap and provide rigidity and turgidity to the cell.
