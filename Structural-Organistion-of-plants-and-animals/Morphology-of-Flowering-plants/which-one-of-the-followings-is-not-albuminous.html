WHICH ONE OF THE FOLLOWINGS IS NOT ALBUMINOUS

(A) MAIZE

(B) WHEAT

(C) BARLEY

(D) ** PEA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct answer is ‘Vallisneria’.

Concept note-2: -Non-albuminous seeds are those which do not have endosperm and they store their food in cotyledons and kernels. They consume their entire endosperm. Example: Almonds and beans. Pea seeds do not have endosperm because they consume it during embryonic development.

Concept note-3: -Non-albuminous seeds are the seeds that do not have residual endosperm because it is completely consumed during embryonic development. For example, pea, groundnut.

Concept note-4: -Assertion :Ex-albuminous seeds do not posses any residual endosperm, as it is completely consumed during embryo development. Reason: Wheat, castor, pea and groundnut are all the examples of ex-albuminous seeds.

Concept note-5: -They are referred to as non-albuminous or non-endospermic seeds.
