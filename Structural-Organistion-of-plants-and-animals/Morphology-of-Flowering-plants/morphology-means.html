MORPHOLOGY OF FLOWERING PLANTS

[SOURCES]
Morphology means

(A) Study of plants

(B) Study of flowers

(C) ** Physics appearance

(D) Non of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Definition: Morphology is the study of shapes, and largerly of what can be seen. It is the study of appearance, as cellular appearance, tissular appearance or tumoral appearance. In biology and medicine, it is the study of the form and structure of organisms and their specific structural features.

Concept note-2: -Morphology, that is, the study of form comprising shape, size, and structure, is important for materials research in general. For nanostructured materials, popularly known as nanomaterials, morphology has special significance since form, in this case, dictates physical and chemical properties [1, 2].

Concept note-3: -Morphology is the study of the internal structure of words and forms a core part of linguistic study today. The term morphology is Greek and is a makeup of morph-meaning ‘shape, form’, and-ology which means ‘the study of something’.

Concept note-4: -Morphology is the study of words and their parts. Morphemes, like prefixes, suffixes and base words, are defined as the smallest meaningful units of meaning.
