What physiological functions are enhanced by epithlial cells bearing microvilli and cilia?

(A) ** increased surface area for absorption & capturing debris

(B) decreased surface area for absorption & capturing debris

(C) increased surface area for absorption of oxygen

(D) decreased surface area for absorption of oxygen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Microvilli on the surface of epithelial cells such as those lining the intestine increase the cellâ€™s surface area and thus facilitate the absorption of ingested food and water molecules.

Concept note-2: -The columnar epithelium of the stomach contains the mucus-secreting cells and in the microvilli, it is highly absorptive as it increases the absorptive surface area. The cells of columnar epithelium are elongated and laced side by side like a column.

Concept note-3: -Microvilli, found in tightly packed arrays on the apical surfaces of polarized epithelial cells, increase the surface area of the cell exposed to the lumen to enhance absorption and secretion.

Concept note-4: -Secretion: Epithelial tissue in your glands (glandular epithelium) can secrete (release) enzymes, hormones and fluids. Absorption: The epithelial lining of your internal organs, such as your liver and lungs, can allow the absorption of certain substances.
