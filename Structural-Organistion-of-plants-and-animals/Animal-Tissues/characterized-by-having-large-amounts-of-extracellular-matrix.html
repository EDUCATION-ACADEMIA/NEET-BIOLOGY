[SOURCES]
Characterized by having large amounts of extracellular matrix.

(A) epithelial.

(B) ** connective.

(C) muscle.

(D) nervous.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Connective tissues are characterized by abundant amounts of extracellular matrix in which a variety of cell types are dispersed. The extracellular matrix between the cells usually includes fibers of one or more types embedded in an amorphous ground substance.

Concept note-2: -The areolar tissue contains the largest quantity of extracellular material. It holds organs in place and attaches epithelial tissue to other underlying tissues.

Concept note-3: -Extracellular Matrix (ECM) It is most abundant in connective tissues such as the basement membrane, bone, tendon, and cartilage, where definition is given to the ECM by the proportions and organization of various components.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: The tissue type characterized by an abundance of extracellular material is B. Connective tissue. Connective tissue has a primary role of structure and support in the body as it helps hold all the cells and tissues together to form organs.

Concept note-5: -Cartilage. Cartilage is a connective tissue with a large amount of the matrix and variable amounts of fibers.
