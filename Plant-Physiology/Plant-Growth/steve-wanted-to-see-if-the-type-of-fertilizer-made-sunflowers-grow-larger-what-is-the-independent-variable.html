(A) ** Type of fertilizer

(B) Size of sunflower

(C) type of sun flower

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The independent variable is plant height and the dependent variable is soil. 3. To test how different types of fertilizer affect plant height, you will add different brands of fertilizer to each plant and measure how tall they grow.

Concept note-2: -In the plant experiment, the independent variable could be fertilizer-some plants will get fertilizer, others will not. The scientists change the amount of the independent variable (the fertilizer) to observe the effects on the dependent variable (plant growth).

Concept note-3: -In this experiment, the addition of fertilizer is the independent variable manipulated by the scientist. Plant height is the dependent variable that responds to the change in the independent variable. Each plant is exposed to an equal amount of sunlight, so sunlight is the control variable.
