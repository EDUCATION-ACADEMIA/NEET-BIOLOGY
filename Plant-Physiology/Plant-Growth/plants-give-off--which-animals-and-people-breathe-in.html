Plants give off ____ which animals and people breathe in.

(A) ** oxygen

(B) carbon dioxide

(C) water

(D) sunlight

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The leftovers from making the plant food is another gas called oxygen. This oxygen is released from the leaves into the air. Take a look at the picture below to see this system in action. This leftover oxygen is really important because humans and other animals need this oxygen to live which we breathe in from the air.

Concept note-2: -Scientists believe that the cow is the only animal that inhales and exhales oxygen, it added.

Concept note-3: -Solution: Animals, during respiration, take in oxygen and release carbon dioxide gas. Plants, on the other hand, utilize this carbon dioxide gas in the process of photosynthesis to produce food and release oxygen in the atmosphere.

Concept note-4: -Exactly, photosynthesis allows the plants, the leaf to take in these ingredients and makes it into food. And so just as we exhale, the stomata also exhale out oxygen. At the same time, they release water.

Concept note-5: -Photosynthesis and respiration are the two essential processes that allow life to sustain on earth. In a way, they are a cycle-plants help humans breathe by providing us with oxygen, and humans help plants “breathe” by providing them with carbon dioxide.
