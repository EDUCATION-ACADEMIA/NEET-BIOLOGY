The substances listed on the left side of a chemical equation are the

(A) Products

(B) Coefficients

(C) Precipitates

(D) ** Reactants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reactants are those substances that are present on the left side in chemical reactions, these are the raw materials that combine in a chemical reaction to form products.

Concept note-2: -The substance(s) to the left of the arrow in a chemical equation are called reactants. A reactant is a substance that is present at the start of a chemical reaction. The substance(s) to the right of the arrow are called products.

Concept note-3: -In a chemical equation, the products are written on the left-hand side and the reactants on the right-hand side with an arrow in between. Q.
