Photosynthesis process is important for humans to ____

(A) create different kinds of plants.

(B) make food

(C) ** produce oxygen we need for breathing.

(D) feed animals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, directly or indirectly, the process of photosynthesis provides most of the energy required by living things on earth. Photosynthesis also results in the release of oxygen into the atmosphere. In short, to eat and breathe, humans depend almost entirely on the organisms that carry out photosynthesis.

Concept note-2: -Thatâ€™s right-more than half of the oxygen you breathe comes from marine photosynthesizers, like phytoplankton and seaweed. Both use carbon dioxide, water and energy from the sun to make food for themselves, releasing oxygen in the process. In other words, they photosynthesize.

Concept note-3: -It takes six molecules of CO2 to produce one molecule of glucose by photosynthesis, and six molecules of oxygen are released as a by-product. Without photosynthesis, animals would never have evolved because the oxygen we need to breathe and survive would not be present in the air or oceans.
