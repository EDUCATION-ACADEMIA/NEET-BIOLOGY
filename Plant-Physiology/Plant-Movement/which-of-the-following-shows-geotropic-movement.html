Which of the following shows geotropic movement

(A) Bending of roots towards Gravity

(B) Bending of stem away from Gravity

(C) ** Both

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The movement of plant parts in response to the direction of gravity is known as geotropism. The growth of plant roots is an example of geotropism as it grows towards the direction of gravity.

Concept note-2: -There are two types of geotropic movement positive geotropism and negative geotropism. If the plant parts move towards the gravitational force, it is known as positive geotropism.

Concept note-3: -Geotropism is directional movement in response to gravity. Charles Darwin was one of the scientists who first showed positive gravitropism in the roots and negative gravitropism in stems.

Concept note-4: -Root shows positive geotropism and shoot shows negative geotropism.
