Germination of pollen grains is the best example for

(A) Photo tropism application of light

(B) Geotropism Gravitation

(C) Hydrotropism is the application of water

(D) Thigmotropism is a tactile application

(E) ** Chemo tropism is a chemical application

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chemotropism is known as growth or movement of a complete plant or plant parts towards the chemical stimulus. For example the growth of pollen tube down to the ovule in the ovary through the stigma and style during fertilization in order to respond to the presence of sugars in the style. Concept note-2: -(iii) Growth of polen tube towards ovum is an example of chemotropism. The growth of the pollen tube toward the ovule responses to the chemicals released by the stigma.

Concept note-3: -Chemotropism is growth in the direction of a chemical stimulus. This type of growth is observed during pollen tube growth in Angiosperms .

Concept note-4: -Plant movement in reaction to chemical stimuli is known as chemotropism. The growth response is positive chemotropism when it is directed toward the stimulus. When the growth response is away from the stimulus, it is called negative chemotropism.
