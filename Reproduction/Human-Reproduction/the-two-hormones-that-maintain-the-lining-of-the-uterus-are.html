The two hormones that maintain the lining of the uterus are:

(A) FSH and Oestrogen

(B) LH and Oestrogen

(C) Oestrogen

(D) ** Progesterone and Oestrogen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The lining of the uterus, or endometrium, prepares each month for the implantation of an embryo. This preparation occurs under the influence of estrogen and progesterone from the ovary.

Concept note-2: -The ovaries maintain the health of the female reproductive system. They secrete two main hormones-estrogen and progesterone.

Concept note-3: -Progesterone and estrogen cause the lining of the uterus to thicken more, to prepare for possible fertilization.

Concept note-4: -The main reproductive hormones estrogen, testosterone, and progesterone are instrumental in sexuality and fertility. They are responsible for pregnancy, puberty, menstruation, menopause, sex drive, sperm production and more.
