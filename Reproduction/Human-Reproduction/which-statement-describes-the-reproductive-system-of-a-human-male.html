Concept note-2: -Urethra. The urethra is the structure that is shared between the male reproductive and urinary systems. In the reproductive system, the urethra plays an essential role in providing a passage for sperm and seminal fluid during ejaculation.

Concept note-3: -The male reproductive system contains the external genitals (the penis, testes and the scrotum) and internal parts, including the prostate gland, vas deferens and urethra.
