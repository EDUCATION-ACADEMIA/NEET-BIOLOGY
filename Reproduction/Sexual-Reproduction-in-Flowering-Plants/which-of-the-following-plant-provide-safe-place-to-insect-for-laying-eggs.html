Which of the following plant provide safe place to insect for laying eggs?

(A) Sage plant

(B) Centaurea

(C) Ophrys

(D) ** Amorphophallus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The flowers of Amorphophallus are pollinated by insects. Pollination by insects is called entomophily . The plant provides a safe place for the insect to lay eggs as a floral reward.

Concept note-2: -The moth deposits its egg in the locule of the ovary and the flowers which are in many feet height intum gets pollinated by the moth. The larvae come out of the egg as the seeds start developing.

Concept note-3: -Solution : Amorphophallus, Fig and Yucca species provides floral rewards in the form of providing safe place to lay eggs.

Concept note-4: -Solution: Amorphophallus provides safe place to insects to lay the eggs.

Concept note-5: -Ants, Sawflies, Wasps, and Bees Sawflies are more common as pollinators.
