Concept note-3: -DNA replication is said to be semi-conservative because of the process of replication, where the resulting double helix is composed of both an old strand and a new strand. Each strand of the double helix DNA would serve as a template for synthesis of a new strand.

Concept note-4: -Meselson and Stahl experiment proved that DNA replicates semi-conservatively, which means that each of its strands acts as a template for the synthesis of a new, complementary strand.
