Which of the following RNAs should be most abundant in animal cell?

(A) t RNA

(B) m RNA

(C) mi RNA

(D) ** r RNA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final answer: The most abundant RNA in animal cells is rRNA.

Concept note-2: -The rRNA is the largest and most abundant RNA in bacterial and archaeal cells. It is also one of the best-characterized RNAs in terms of its structural motifs and sequence variation. Production of ribosome components including >50 ribosomal proteins (r-proteins) consumes significant cellular resources.

Concept note-3: -rRNA was once known to be the key structural component of ribosomes, but its actually found to be a catalytic element for protein synthesis. It is the most abundant type of RNA (about 80%) in the cell. rRNA is comprised of a large and small subunit.

Concept note-4: -Ribosomal RNA also called rRNA provides the site for protein synthesis.It forms 80% of the total RNA content of a cell and hence is the most abundant RNA.
