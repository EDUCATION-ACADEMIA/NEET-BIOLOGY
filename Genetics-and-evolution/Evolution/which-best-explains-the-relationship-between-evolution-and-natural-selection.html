Which best explains the relationship between evolution and natural selection?

(A) Natural selection prevents evolution.

(B) Evolution prevents natural selection.

(C) Evolution causes natural selection.

(D) ** Natural selection is one aspect of evolution.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Natural selection can lead to speciation, where one species gives rise to a new and distinctly different species. It is one of the processes that drives evolution and helps to explain the diversity of life on Earth.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Evolution is a process, and natural selection is the mechanism. Evolution describes the process of a population becoming more adapted to its environment over time. Evolution occurs through the mechanism of natural selection.

Concept note-3: -Which best describes the relationship between evolution and natural selection? Natural selection leads to evolution.

Concept note-4: -Natural selection is a mechanism (differential reproductive success due to traits) Evolution is a process (change in allele frequencies over time) Natural selection is one mechanism that can lead to evolution.

Concept note-5: -So, the correct answer is ‘Individuals with the most favorable traits survive and reproduce’
