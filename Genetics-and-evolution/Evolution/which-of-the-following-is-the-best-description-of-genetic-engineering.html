Which of the following is the best description of genetic engineering?

(A) The survival of the fittest organisms because they are the best adapted to their habitat

(B) Only allowing the organisms with the desired characteristics to breed and produce offspring

(C) ** Changing the genome of one organism by introducing a gene from a different organism in order to produce a particular characteristic

(D) Cloning a desirable tomato plant by using meristem cells

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A transgenic, or genetically modified, organism is one that has been altered through recombinant DNA technology, which involves either the combining of DNA from different genomes or the insertion of foreign DNA into a genome.

Concept note-2: -Definition. Genetic engineering (also called genetic modification) is a process that uses laboratory-based technologies to alter the DNA makeup of an organism. This may involve changing a single base pair (A-T or C-G), deleting a region of DNA or adding a new segment of DNA.

Concept note-3: -Genetic modification Often GM involves isolating and removing the DNA encoding a single gene from one organism, manipulating it outside the cell (in a laboratory) and reinserting it into the same organism or into the genetic material of another organism.

Concept note-4: -Genetic engineering is also called genetic modification or GM. It involves modifying the genome of an organism by identifying and removing a gene from the genome of one organism, and introducing it into the genome of another organism to result in a desired characteristic.

Concept note-5: -Genetic engineering is the modification of an organism’s phenotype by manipulating its genetic material. Some genetic engineering uses the principle of recombination.
