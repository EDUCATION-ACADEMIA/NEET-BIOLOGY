Which of the following has to do with your actions being because of you genetics?

(A) ** Nature

(B) Nurture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some characteristics are biologically determined, such as eye color, hair color, and skin color. Other things, like life expectancy and height, have a strong biological component but are also influenced by environmental factors and lifestyle.

Concept note-2: -Eye color and skin pigmentation are examples of “nature” because they are present at birth and determined by inherited genes.

Concept note-3: -In summary, based on several studies and research it can be concluded that human behaviour is both nature and nurture. In addition, evidence also supports that animal behaviour specifically (grizzly bears) is also due to nature and nurture.

Concept note-4: -Nature is what we think of as pre-wiring and is influenced by genetic inheritance and other biological factors. Nurture is generally taken as the influence of external factors after conception, e.g., the product of exposure, life experiences and learning on an individual.
