Which of the following is the best statement about natural selection?

(A) ** Some individuals in a population have a variation that gives them an advantage to survive and reproduce

(B) a.Most populations of organisms evolve and become more and more alike, eventually becoming genetic clones.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, natural selection favors the individual with survival and/or reproductive advantage over others under existing environmental condition. Accordingly, variation is present in the population. Thus, the correct answer is option E.

Concept note-2: -So, the correct answer is ‘Individuals with the most favorable traits survive and reproduce’

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is E) It requires genetic variation, results in descent with modification, and involves differential reproductive success.

Concept note-4: -Therefore, the correct option is B. Individuals within a population exhibit genetic variation and those that are best adapted to their environment will survive.

Concept note-5: -Natural selection describes an individual becoming better suited to its environment; evolution describes a population becoming better suited to its environment. D. Natural selection and evolution are the same thing. The correct answer option is (B).
