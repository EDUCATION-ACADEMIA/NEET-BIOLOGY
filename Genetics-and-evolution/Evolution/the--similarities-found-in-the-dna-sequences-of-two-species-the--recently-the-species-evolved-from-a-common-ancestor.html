[SOURCES]
The ____ similarities found in the DNA sequences of two species the ____ recently the species evolved from a common ancestor.

(A) more, less

(B) ** more, more

(C) less, more

(D) less, less

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -That’s because closely related species most likely diverged from one another fairly recently in the evolutionary span. Thus, they haven’t had as much time to accumulate random mutations in their genetic codes.

Concept note-2: -Evolutionarily Related Gene Sequences Homologous genes can be generated by speciation, which produces pairs of orthologs (genes in different species that are derived from the same gene in the last common ancestral species, and thus usually have similar functions).

Concept note-3: -The pattern of branching in a phylogenetic tree reflects how species or other groups evolved from a series of common ancestors. In trees, two species are more related if they have a more recent common ancestor and less related if they have a less recent common ancestor.

Concept note-4: -common ancestor: The most recent ancestral form or species from which two different species evolved. comparative biology: The study of patterns among more than one species.
