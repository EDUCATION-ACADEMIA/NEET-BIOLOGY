Select of all of the genotypes which are homozygous from the options below.

(A) Aa

(B) ** DD

(C) ** tt

(D) Gg

(E) Ff

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An organism can be either homozygous dominant ( TT ) or homozygous recessive ( tt ). If an organism has two different alleles ( Tt ) for a certain gene, it is known as heterozygous (hetero-means different).

Concept note-2: -The presence of two identical alleles at a particular gene locus. A homozygous genotype may include two normal alleles or two alleles that have the same variant.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer: The genotype which shows the character of homozygous dominant is D. SS.

Concept note-4: -If there are two alleles in a population at locus A (A and a), then the possible genotypes in that population are AA, Aa, and aa. Individuals with genotypes AA and aa are homozygotes (i.e., they have two copies of the same allele).
