Which sentences are FALSE?

(A) Humans are made up of 23 pairs of chromosomes.

(B) Human are made up of 46 chromosomes.

(C) ** Chromosomes is made up of cytoplasm.

(D) Chromosomes is located in the nucleus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: False. Chromosome are not found in the cytoplasm. Chromosomes are found in the nucleus of the cell.

Concept note-2: -Protoplasm is considered as the physical basis of life. The protoplasm of a cell consists of a nucleus, cell membrane, and the cytoplasm. Thus, cytoplasm is a part of the protoplasm of a cell.

Concept note-3: -Cytoplasm Cytoplasm is the gelatinous liquid that fills the inside of a cell. It is composed of water, salts, and various organic molecules. Some intracellular organelles, such the nucleus and mitochondria, are enclosed by membranes that separate them from the cytoplasm.

Concept note-4: -Each of these cells is a discrete structure surrounded by a cell membrane and filled with a thick solution called cytoplasm. In human cells, most DNA is found in a compartment within the cell called a nucleus.
