EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Inheritance refers to the assets that an individual bequeaths to their loved ones after they pass away. An inheritance may contain cash, investments such as stocks or bonds, and other assets such as jewelry, automobiles, art, antiques, and real estate.

Concept note-2: -Beneficiary: Someone named in a legal document to inherit money or other property. Wills, trusts, and insurance policies commonly name beneficiaries; beneficiaries can also be named for “payable-on-death” accounts. Bequeath: To leave property at one’s death; another word for “give."

Concept note-3: -Inheritance is the process by which genetic information is passed on from parent to child. This is why members of the same family tend to have similar characteristics.
