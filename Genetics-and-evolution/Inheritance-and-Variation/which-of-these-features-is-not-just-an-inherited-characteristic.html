Which of these features is NOT just an inherited characteristic?

(A) ** skin colour

(B) eye colour

(C) blood group

(D) natural hair colour

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final answer: Colour of the eye, skin color, and nature of hair is inherited characteristics but the size of the body is an acquired character.

Concept note-2: -Polygenic Trait A polygenic trait is a characteristic, such as height or skin color, that is influenced by two or more genes. Because multiple genes are involved, polygenic traits do not follow the patterns of Mendelian inheritance.

Concept note-3: -Final Answer: Thus, the size of the body is the character that can be acquired but not inherited.
