Which of the following is in the heterozygous form?

(A) AA

(B) ** Aa

(C) aa

(D) HT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A heterozygous individual has two different alleles. Therefore it would have the genotype Aa. An individual with the genotype Aa can make two types of gametes: A and a. Since this is a random process, the individual will, on average, make equal numbers of each gamete. (Frequency of 1/2 for each gamete).

Concept note-2: -If an organism has two copies of the same allele, for example AA or aa, it is homozygous for that trait. If the organism has one copy of two different alleles, for example Aa, it is heterozygous.

Concept note-3: -This is the case with Huntington disease, achondroplastic dwarfism, and polydactyly. People who are heterozygous (Aa) are not healthy carriers. They have the disorder just like homozygous dominant (AA) individuals. of being entirely normal.

Concept note-4: -Aa-heterozygous dominant (individual has both dominant and recessive gene, but only one is expressed) Was this answer helpful?
