whats a pedigree?

(A) ** in genetics a type of family tree that maps the occurrence of a trait in a family

(B) A contract

(C) written description of all genes worldwide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A pedigree shows relationships between family members and indicates which individuals have certain genetic pathogenic variants, traits, and diseases within a family as well as vital status. A pedigree can be used to determine disease inheritance patterns within a family. Standard pedigree nomenclature.

Concept note-2: -… A pedigree, as related to genetics, is a chart that diagrams the inheritance of a trait or health condition through generations of a family. The pedigree particularly shows the relationships among family members and, when the information is available, indicates which individuals have a trait(s) of interest.

Concept note-3: -What is Pedigree Analysis? Pedigree analysis was developed to understand the inheritance of genes from parents to offspring. It was developed as a chart that can represent a family tree along with the family members and their genetic traits, respectively.

Concept note-4: -A family tree, also called a genealogy or a pedigree chart, is a chart representing family relationships in a conventional tree structure. More detailed family trees, used in medicine and social work, are known as genograms.

Concept note-5: -The difference between the family tree and the pedigree chart is that the pedigree chart is generally a direct lineage chart that shows only your parents, grandparents, great grandparents, etc. No siblings, aunts, cousins, or Grandpa’s brother Bob.
